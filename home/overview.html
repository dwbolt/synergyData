The Synergy Project came out of the desire for Sustainable Future Center to create synergy among it's donors, supporters, volunteers, and partner organizations. The intent is to make the synergy software available to other non-profits as it matures. Currently, the synergy software does:

  1. website Google Folder
  2. accounting
  3. calendar doc
We plan to expand functionality into management of:
  1. donars
  2. supporters
  3. volunteers
  4. projects
